about me

Hello, my name is Rodrigo, I am 22 years old. I am from Granada (Spain) and two years ago I began to develop my career as a web developer. I began my studies self-taught although today I study at the Francisco Ayala Institute.

I am passionate about the world of development and more than a hobby, it is a vocation for me.

Currently, my main working tools are HTML, CSS and JavaScript on the client side, while on the server side, I specialize in PHP. I have worked on various projects, from static websites to dynamic web applications, which has allowed me to gain experience in fullstack development.

In my daily life, I use frameworks like React.js to build interactive and attractive user interfaces. Additionally, I have worked with Laravel and Symfony to build efficient server-side web servers. In the field of databases, I have used MongoDB and MySQL, adapting to the specific needs of each project.

My approach in web development focuses on creating intuitive and engaging user experiences. I strive to stay up to date with the latest trends and technologies in the web world, actively participating in online communities and attending development-related events and conferences.
